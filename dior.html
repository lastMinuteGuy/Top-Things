Dior

Dior is a French luxury brand founded in 1946 by Christian Dior. Famous for its couture fashion and perfumes like Sauvage and Miss Dior. Dior blends classic elegance with contemporary style across its lines. It is a symbol of timeless luxury and sophistication.
