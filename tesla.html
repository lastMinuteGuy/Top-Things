Tesla

Tesla, Inc. is an American electric vehicle and clean energy company founded in 2003. Led by Elon Musk, Tesla revolutionized the car industry with electric models like the Model S, Model 3, and Cybertruck. Tesla also pioneers battery storage, solar panels, and autonomous driving technologies. Their mission is to accelerate the world’s transition to sustainable energy.
