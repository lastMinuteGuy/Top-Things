The Dark Knight

The Dark Knight is a 2008 superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan. It features Christian Bale as Batman and Heath Ledger in his legendary performance as the Joker. Known for its dark tone and complex characters, The Dark Knight is a benchmark in superhero storytelling. It received multiple awards and set new standards for comic-book adaptations.
