Lamborghini

Automobili Lamborghini is an Italian brand producing extravagant high-performance cars. Founded by Ferruccio Lamborghini in 1963, the company is known for bold designs and powerful engines. Models like the Aventador, Huracán, and Urus SUV represent performance and luxury. Lamborghini competes directly with Ferrari and McLaren in the exotic supercar market. The raging bull logo embodies strength and passion.
