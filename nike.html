Nike

Nike is one of the world's largest suppliers of athletic shoes and apparel. Founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports, it became Nike, Inc. in 1971. Nike is globally known for its innovative designs, athletic sponsorships, and high-performance footwear. Their "Just Do It" slogan has become iconic worldwide. Nike remains a leader in sportswear, streetwear, and performance gear across the globe.
