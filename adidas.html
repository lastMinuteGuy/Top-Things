Adidas

Adidas is a German multinational corporation founded in 1949 by Adolf Dassler. It specializes in the design and manufacture of shoes, clothing, and accessories. Known for the three stripes logo, Adidas has a huge influence in soccer, running, and fashion markets. They sponsor leading athletes and teams around the world. Their Ultraboost shoes and Originals collection are world famous.
