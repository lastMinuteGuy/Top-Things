Avengers

The Avengers is a 2012 superhero film produced by Marvel Studios. It brings together heroes like Iron Man, Thor, Captain America, and Hulk in one universe. Directed by Joss Whedon, the film was groundbreaking in creating the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). It achieved massive critical acclaim and box office success.
