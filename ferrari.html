Ferrari

Ferrari is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939. Ferrari is synonymous with speed, luxury, and elite engineering. It is a dominant force in Formula One racing and automotive design innovation. Famous models include the LaFerrari, 812 Superfast, and F8 Tributo. Ferrari’s prancing horse logo symbolizes power, prestige, and exclusivity.
