Inception

Inception is a 2010 science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The movie explores the possibility of shared dreaming and implanting ideas into the subconscious. Starring Leonardo DiCaprio, it became a major success both critically and financially. Its unique storytelling and layered dream sequences set it apart as a cinematic masterpiece.
